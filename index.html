Tips & Guidance

🌅 Before You Start

  • Drink a glass of water before leaving home
  • Wear supportive, closed-toe sneakers
  • Let Bay Club staff know you're new — they love helping!
  • Never skip the warm-up, even if you feel great

⚙️ Using the Machines

  • Always adjust the seat first — good alignment protects your joints
  • Start lighter than you think — you can always go heavier next week
  • Move slowly and with control — no jerking or dropping weights
  • Breathe out on the push or pull, in on the way back
  • Rest 60–90 seconds between sets — this is not wasted time!

💛 Good to Know

  • Mild muscle soreness 1–2 days after is completely normal
  • If something feels sharp in a joint, skip it today
  • Strength training improves bone density, balance & energy
  • 3 days a week is perfect — rest days are when you get stronger!

🥗 Food & Recovery

  • Have a protein snack within 1 hour after training (yogurt, eggs, cheese)
  • Aim for 6–8 glasses of water throughout the day
  • Sleep is when muscles rebuild — aim for 7–8 hours
  • A gentle walk on rest days keeps things loose without overdoing it

🏆 Remember This

Getting yourself to the gym is the biggest win. Every single rep you complete is building a stronger, more independent future. You are doing something remarkable — be proud every single time you walk through those doors. 💚
